Pork Adobo

Description

Pork adobo is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons garlic (minced/crushed)
  • 5 dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablesppons vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorn
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic. Then marinade for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat pot and put in marinated pork belly, then cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour remaining marinade including garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil.
  5. Put in vinegar and simmer for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
